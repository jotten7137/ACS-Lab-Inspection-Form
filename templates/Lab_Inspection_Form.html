Chemical Safety

# Question Yes No N/A Remarks/Notes
1 Appropriate labels are found on all hazardous chemical containers?
2 Containers are in good condition (e.g., labels intact, metal cans free of rust) and closed when not in use?
3 Containers properly segregated by hazard class (e.g., flammables away from oxidizers, acids separate from bases, incompatible acids separated)?
4 Storage of chemicals above eye level is avoided?
5 Flammable liquids stored in OSHA/NFPA approved cabinets and safety containers?
6 Flammables liquids requiring refrigeration stored in either explosion proof or flammable resistant refrigerators and freezers (i.e., no regular refrigerators)?
7 Ignition sources avoided when using/storing flammables?
8 Corrosives stored in acid cabinets or other appropriate cabinets?
9 Peroxide formers properly labeled and inventory tracked?
10 Large containers (4L+) stored near the floor?
11 Bottle carriers or carts utilized when transporting hazardous chemicals between work areas?
12 Proper signs delineate designated areas where high hazard chemicals are used?
13 Designated chemical work areas/bench tops cleaned and decontaminated?

Compressed Gases

# Question Yes No N/A Remarks/Notes
1 Cylinders stored upright and properly secured at all times?
2 Caps properly secured when cylinders are not in use??
3 Regulators always used, proper regulators used for type gas, pressure bled when not in use??
4 Cylinders in good condition and clearly marked?
5 Flammables stored separately from oxidizers, toxics in secure area, etc.?
6 Cylinders of flammable gases stored in ventilated enclosures?
7 Cylinders moved on cylinder trucks with regulators removed and caps secured?
8 Cylinders of toxic gases (e.g., NFPA health hazard 3 or 4 and 2) stored and used in continuously ventilated enclosures?
9 Cryogenic gas cylinder pressure relief values in proper working condition?
10 Oxygen monitor available in areas with increased likelihood of oxygen deficient atmospheres?

General Safety

# Question Yes No N/A Remarks/Notes
1 Lab is secure when door is locked and no one is in the lab?
2 Appropriate warning signs posted near lab entrance?
3 Unobstructed aisles maintained at least 36in. wide throughout work space?
